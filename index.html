Our story

Started by dog people, for dog people.

Hearth & Hound began with a senior beagle named Biscuit and a simple frustration: why was it so hard to find pet products that were genuinely good for dogs and beautiful enough to live with?

So we built the brand we wished existed — comfort-first, vet-informed, and obsessively tested by the pickiest critics around: our own dogs. Every product earns its place by making a real difference in a dog's day.
